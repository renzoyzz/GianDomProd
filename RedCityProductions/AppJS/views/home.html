ABOUT
Film Forest is a film production and new media company. We create high quality, professional level media in multiple formats. From commercials & marketing ads, to music videos & event coverage, to movies & documentaries, Film Forest carries every project from inception to completion. Able to make any budget work we shoot projects that run the gamut of production sizes. Film Forest shoots can range from small, one-man-band projects, all the way up to large format, fully staffed, multiple camera productions.
Originally conceived in Miami, Film Forest works and shoots all over the country. Created by a group of close friends and industry professionals, the crew at Film Forest has over ten years of production experience. Our experience in the industry has given us the veteran-level production knowledge that is needed for seeing complex projects into successful completion.
